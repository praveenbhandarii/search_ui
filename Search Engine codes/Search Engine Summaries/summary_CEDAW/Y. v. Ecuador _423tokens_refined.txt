- Case Summary: The author, Y, submitted a communication to the Committee on the Elimination of Discrimination against Women alleging discrimination by Ecuador. The Committee decided to discontinue its consideration of the case as the author had been granted a MERCOSUR permanent residence permit in Ecuador and no longer risked deportation.

Decisions and Rulings:

Impact and Significance:
