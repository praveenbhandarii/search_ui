Case ID: ICJ/155
Court/Tribunal: International Court of Justice (ICJ)
Document Date: 2014
Key Parties Involved: Republic of Nicaragua, Republic of Colombia
Case Name: Alleged Violations of Sovereign Rights and Maritime Spaces in the Caribbean Sea (Nicaragua v. Colombia)

Case Summary:
Nicaragua filed an Application on November 26, 2013, instituting proceedings against Colombia before the ICJ. The dispute revolves around the violations of Nicaragua's sovereign rights and maritime zones, as declared by the ICJ's Judgment on November 19, 2012, in the case concerning Territorial and Maritime Dispute (Nicaragua v. Colombia). Nicaragua also alleges that Colombia has threatened to use force to implement these violations.
