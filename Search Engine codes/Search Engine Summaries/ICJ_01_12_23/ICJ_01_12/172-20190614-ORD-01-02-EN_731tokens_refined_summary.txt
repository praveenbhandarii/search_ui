Case ID: ICJ/172
Court/Tribunal: International Court of Justice (ICJ)
Document Date: 2019
Key Parties Involved: Qatar, United Arab Emirates (UAE)
Case Name: Application of the International Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Racial Discrimination (Qatar v. United Arab Emirates)

Case Summary:
Qatar filed a case against the UAE on June 11, 2018, alleging violations of the International Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Racial Discrimination (CERD). This document is an order from the ICJ regarding the case.

Context:
- The dispute between Qatar and the UAE is related to the application of CERD.
- The court lacks jurisdiction.
- The alleged rights of the UAE are not plausibly based on CERD.
- The court should have analyzed the UAE's claims before reaching a conclusion on the request for provisional measures.

Citations: None provided in the document.
