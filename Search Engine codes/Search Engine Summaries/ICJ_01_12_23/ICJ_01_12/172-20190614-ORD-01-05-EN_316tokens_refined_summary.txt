Key Parties Involved: Qatar, United Arab Emirates (UAE)
Case Name: Application of the International Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Racial Discrimination (Qatar v. United Arab Emirates)

Case Summary:
Qatar filed a case against the UAE on 11 June 2018, alleging violations of the International Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Racial Discrimination (CERD). This document is an order from the ICJ regarding the case.

Context:
- Case number: 1170
- Date of the order: 23 July 2018
- Judge Nawaf Salam is involved in the case and maintains his position that the court lacks jurisdiction.
- Judge Salam emphasizes the need for the parties to avoid actions that could worsen or prolong the dispute.
- Judge Salam rejected the court's order indicating provisional measures due to the lack of jurisdiction.
- The document is labeled as "401 Declaration of Judge Salam" and includes the date "23 July 2018."
