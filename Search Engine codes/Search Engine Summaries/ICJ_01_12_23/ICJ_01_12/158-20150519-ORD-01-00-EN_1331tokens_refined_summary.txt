Case ID: ICJ/158
Court/Tribunal: International Court of Justice (ICJ)
Document Date: 2015
Key Parties Involved: Republic of the Marshall Islands (Complainant), Republic of India (Defendant)
Case Name: Obligations concerning Negotiations relating to Cessation of the Nuclear Arms Race and to Nuclear Disarmament (Marshall Islands v. India)

Case Summary:
The Republic of the Marshall Islands filed Applications against nine States, including India, accusing them of not fulfilling their obligations regarding the cessation of the nuclear arms race and nuclear disarmament. This document pertains to the jurisdiction of the ICJ to hear the case.

Context:
- The Republic of the Marshall Islands filed the Applications on April 24, 2014.

- The legal issue in question is the jurisdiction of the ICJ to hear the case.
- The Memorial of the Marshall Islands and the CounterMemorial of India are related documents.
- Key dates include the timelimits for filing the Memorial and CounterMemorial, as well as an extended timelimit for the CounterMemorial.
- The Judges involved in the case include President Abraham, Vice President Yusuf, and several other Judges.
- Registrar Couvreur is also involved in the case.
- The document is available in English and French, with the English text being authoritative.
- Citations include Article 48 of the Statute of the Court and Article 44, paragraph 3, of the Rules of Court.
- The ICJ extended the timelimit for filing the CounterMemorial of India to September 16, 2015, and reserved the subsequent procedure for further decision.
