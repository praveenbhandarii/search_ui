Case ID: ICJ/174
Court/Tribunal: International Court of Justice (ICJ)
Document Date: 2019
Key Parties Involved: Kingdom of Bahrain, Arab Republic of Egypt, United Arab Emirates (Applicants) v. State of Qatar (Respondent)
Case Name: Appeal relating to the Jurisdiction of the ICAO Council under Article II, Section 2, of the 1944 International Air Services Transit Agreement (Bahrain, Egypt and United Arab Emirates v. Qatar)

Case Summary:
This legal document is an order from the International Court of Justice (ICJ) in Case Number 174. The case involves an appeal filed by the Kingdom of Bahrain, the Arab Republic of Egypt, and the United Arab Emirates against the State of Qatar. The appeal is related to the jurisdiction of the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) Council under Article II, Section 2, of the 1944 International Air Services Transit Agreement. The appeal was filed in response to a decision rendered by the ICAO Council on 29 June 2018 in proceedings initiated by Qatar against the four states.

Context:
- The appeal was filed on 4 July 2018.
- The ICAO Council's decision was made on 29 June 2018.
- The time-limits for filing a Memorial by the Applicants and a Counter-Memorial by the Respondent were fixed on 25 July 2018.
- The Memorial was filed by the Applicants on 27 December 2018.
- The Counter-Memorial was filed by the Respondent on 25 February 2019.
- The document was signed on 22 January 2020.
