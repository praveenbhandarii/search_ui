Combined Summary:

Case Details:
- Case ID: E/C.12/70/D/88/2018
- Court/Tribunal: United Nations Committee on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights (UNCESCR)
- Document Date: 2021
- Key Parties Involved: M.V.G.D., M.D.E.G. and G.D.G.G. (authors) vs. Spain (State party)
- Case Name: M.V.G.D., M.D.E.G. and G.D.G.G. v. Spain
- Case Summary: The authors alleged that Spain violated their right to adequate housing under Article 11(1) of the International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights after being evicted from their dwelling following termination of their lease.

Procedural History:
- The authors submitted the communication on November 9, 2018, and the Committee registered it without requesting interim measures from the State party.
- The Committee noted that the authors failed to respond to repeated requests for comments on the State party's observations.
- The Committee decided to discontinue its consideration of the communication at its 70th session (September 27 - October 15, 2021) in accordance with Rule 17 of its provisional rules of procedure under the Optional Protocol.

Legal Analysis and Reasoning:
- Submitted by: M.V.G.D., M.D.E.G. and G.D.G.G.
- Procedural issue: Failure to respond to repeated requests for comments on the State party's observations
- Substantive issue: Right to adequate housing
- Subject matter: Eviction from a dwelling following termination of the lease
- Article of the Covenant: 11 (1)

Decisions and Rulings:
- The Committee decided to discontinue its consideration of the communication at its 70th session (September 27 - October 15, 2021) in accordance with Rule 17 of its provisional rules of procedure under the Optional Protocol.

Impact and Significance:
- The discontinuation of the consideration of the communication by the Committee has implications for the authors' case and the broader legal impact on the right to adequate housing under the International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights.

Citations and References:
- Decision adopted by the Committee under the Optional Protocol to the International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights, concerning communication No. 88/2018
