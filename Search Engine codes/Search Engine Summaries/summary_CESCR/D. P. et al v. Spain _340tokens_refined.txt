Combined Summary:

Case Details:
- Case ID: E/C.12/66/D/38/2018
- Court/Tribunal: United Nations Committee on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights (UNCESCR)
- Document Dates: 2019
- Key Parties Involved:
  - D.P. and others (authors)
  - Spain (State party)
- Case Name: D.P. and others v. Spain
- Case Summary: The authors, who were evicted from their homes without legal title, submitted a communication to the UNCESCR alleging that their rights under the International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights had been violated. The Committee considered the communication and decided to discontinue its consideration due to the withdrawal of the case by the authors.

Procedural History:
- The communication was submitted on June 15, 2018
- The Committee met on October 11, 2019, to consider the communication
- The decision was adopted by the Committee at its sixtysixth session, which took place from September 30 to October 18, 2019
- The document is dated November 19, 2019, and was distributed on November 27, 2019

Legal Analysis and Reasoning:
- Article of the Covenant: Not specified
- Submitted by: D.P. and others
- Procedural issue: Withdrawal of the case by the authors
- Substantive issue: Eviction for occupation without legal title
- Subject matter: Housing

Decisions and Rulings:
- The Committee decided to discontinue its consideration of communication No. 38/2018
- The decision was made in accordance with article 17 of the Committee's provisional rules of procedure under the Optional Protocol to the International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights
- The reason for the decision was that the authors had withdrawn the case, stating that their housing situation had changed substantially for reasons unrelated to the procedure

Impact and Significance:
- The decision sets a precedent for cases involving evictions for occupation without legal title under the International Covenant on Economic, Social and Cultural Rights
- It highlights the importance of procedural issues and the withdrawal of cases by the authors in the Committee's decision-making process
