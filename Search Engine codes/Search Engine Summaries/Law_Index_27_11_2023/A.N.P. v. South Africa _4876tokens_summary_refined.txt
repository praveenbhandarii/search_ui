Case Details:

- Case Name: A.N.P. v. South Africa
- Case ID: CRPD/C/23/D/73/2019
- Court/Tribunal: Committee on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (CRPD)
- Document Dates: March 2019
- Key Parties Involved:
  - A.N.P. (the author and victim)
  - South Africa (the state party)

Document Details:

- The document is a communication submitted to the Committee on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (CRPD) by A.N.P., alleging violations of the Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities by South Africa.
- The author claims that the State party denied his requests for tax rebates under the rates rebate program for disabled persons and senior citizens, and that the City of Cape Town's decisions on his tax rebate claims were unlawful, unreasonable, and discriminatory.
- The author alleges violations of several articles of the Convention, including the right to equal access to support required in exercising legal capacity, the right to equal right to own or inherit property, and the right to access to justice.
