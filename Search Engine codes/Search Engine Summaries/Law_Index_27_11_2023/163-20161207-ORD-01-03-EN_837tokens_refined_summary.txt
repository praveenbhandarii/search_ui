Title: Immunities and Criminal Proceedings (Equatorial Guinea v. France)

Case Overview:
Case Number: 163
On 13 June 2016, the Republic of Equatorial Guinea initiated proceedings against the French Republic regarding a dispute concerning the immunity from criminal jurisdiction of the Second Vice-President of Equatorial Guinea, Mr. Teodoro Nguema Obiang Mangue, and the legal status of the building housing the Embassy of Equatorial Guinea in France.
