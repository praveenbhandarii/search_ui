Title: Maritime Delimitation in the Indian Ocean (Somalia v. Kenya)

Case Overview:
The case involves a dispute between Somalia and Kenya regarding the delimitation of maritime spaces in the Indian Ocean. Somalia filed an Application with the International Court of Justice (ICJ) on August 28, 2014, seeking the determination of the maritime boundary between the two states based on international law.

- The proceedings on the merits are suspended until the resolution of the timelimit for Somalia's written statement.
