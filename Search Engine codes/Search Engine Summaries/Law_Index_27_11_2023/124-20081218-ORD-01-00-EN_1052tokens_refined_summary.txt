Title: Territorial and Maritime Dispute (Nicaragua v. Colombia)

Case Overview:
The case involves a territorial and maritime dispute between Nicaragua and Colombia. Nicaragua filed an Application in 2001, initiating proceedings against Colombia. The dispute revolves around legal issues related to territory and maritime delimitation. Colombia filed preliminary objections to jurisdiction, leading to a suspension of the proceedings on the merits.

Important Details:

- Nicaragua requested a one-year time limit to prepare its Reply.

- Timelimits for filing pleadings were set as follows: Nicaragua's Reply by September 18, 2009, and Colombia's Rejoinder by June 18, 2010.
