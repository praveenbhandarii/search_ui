  "Case Summary": "Somalia filed an Application with the International Court of Justice (ICJ) on 28 August 2014, initiating proceedings against Kenya. The dispute concerns the delimitation of maritime spaces claimed by both states in the Indian Ocean. Somalia requested the Court to determine the complete course of the single maritime boundary dividing all the maritime areas belonging to Somalia and Kenya in the Indian Ocean, including the continental shelf beyond 200 nautical miles. The ICJ fixed timelimits for the filing of written pleadings: 13 July 2015 for Somalia's memorial and 27 May 2016 for Kenya's Countermemorial.
